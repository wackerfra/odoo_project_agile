Agile Sprint Management & Reports

Scrum Methodology for Odoo Project

This add-on extends Odoo's project management with essential agile methodologies. It provides professional sprint management, specialized planning boards, and a suite of analytical reports like Burndown, Burnup, Velocity, and CFD.

Sprint Planning & Board

Efficiently plan your sprints with a specialized Kanban view. Manage your Product Backlog and drag tasks into Planned or Active sprints. Track your team's day-to-day progress on the Active Sprint Board.

  • Dedicated Sprint Planning Board.
  • Backlog management column.
  • Real-time sprint metrics.

Professional Agile Reporting

Gain deep insights into your team's performance with industry-standard agile charts. Track hours and task counts across multiple dimensions.

  • Burndown & Burnup: Daily progress tracking.
  • Sprint Velocity: Completed vs Committed hours.
  • Cumulative Flow (CFD): Stage distribution over time.
  • Lead & Cycle Time: Performance bottlenecks analysis.

Key Features

Sprint Management

Planned, Active, and Closed sprint states with capacity tracking.

Advanced Charts

Native integration with Odoo's reporting engine for agile metrics.

Timesheet Integration

Automatic tracking of consumed hours via timesheets within the sprint window.

Developed for Odoo 19 Community Edition

Fully compatible with Odoo 19.0. Professional quality, clean code, and ready for your production environment.
